Wombat

Wombats are chunky little vegetarian animals that live only in Australia and Tasmania. They are marsupials, which means that they carry their babies in a pouch after they are born. Wombats are excellent diggers and create tunnels and burrows to live in. Their digestive systems are so slow it can take them up to two weeks to digest their food. Although wombats look like easy targets, they have tough backsides that they can use to block predators from eating them.
